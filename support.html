Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a script?

From the productions screen, tap the add button and choose to import via camera, text file, or by typing it in manually. The camera option scans a physical page and extracts the text automatically.

What does OffBook Pro unlock?

OffBook Pro is a one-off purchase (no subscription) that unlocks confidence scoring and statistics, unlimited productions, camera script scanning, and the ability to import shared .offbook files. Everyone gets a free trial of these features to try before buying.

Does OffBook work without an internet connection?

Yes, for the most part. Script storage, rehearsal, and line-hiding all work fully offline. A few features use the internet: AI script import and camera scanning send your script to Google's Gemini service for the most accurate results (with an on-device fallback when you're offline), and on Android voice recognition uses Google's speech recognition service. On iOS, voice recognition uses Apple's Speech framework, which may run on-device or via Apple's servers depending on your device and language.

I bought Pro but it's not unlocking. What do I do?

First, try closing and reopening the app. If that doesn't help, check that you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used to purchase. Still stuck? Email me at the address above with your order/receipt details and I'll sort it out.

Can I use OffBook on more than one device?

Your Pro purchase is tied to your Apple ID or Google account, so it should carry across any device you're signed into with that same account. Your script and rehearsal data, however, is stored locally on each device and doesn't currently sync between them.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled directly by Apple or Google, not by OffBook. For iOS purchases, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Android, refunds can be requested through the Google Play Store's order history.

I've found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email the address above with as much detail as you can — what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what happened instead. Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot too.

Can you add [feature]?

Maybe! OffBook's feature list is shaped almost entirely by feedback from performers actually using it. Send your idea over and I'll genuinely consider it.
