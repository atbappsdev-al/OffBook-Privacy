Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
OffBook is built with a simple principle: your data is yours. We don't ask you to create an account, and we don't use any analytics, advertising, or tracking. Your productions, scripts, and rehearsal history live on your device. The only time your content leaves your device is when you choose to use an optional AI feature — importing or scanning a script — which sends that content to Google for processing, as explained below.

1. Information We Collect

OffBook does not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details.

All data you create within the app — including productions, scripts, characters, rehearsal sessions, and confidence scores — is stored locally on your device. The one exception is the optional AI-powered script import and camera scanning features: when you use them, the specific script content you choose to import or scan is sent to Google for processing. This is described in Section 4.

2. Camera Permission

OffBook requests access to your device's camera solely to allow you to import scripts by photographing a physical document. This is an optional feature.

When your device is online, the captured page images are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to extract and format the text — this produces the most accurate result and is described in more detail in Section 4. If you are offline, or if the AI service is unavailable, OffBook instead extracts the text on your device using on-device text recognition (Google ML Kit on Android, Apple Vision on iOS), with no upload.

In either case, captured images are used only to extract the text. OffBook does not save them to your photo library and does not retain them after the text has been extracted.

3. Microphone Permission

OffBook requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable the line rehearsal feature, which allows you to speak your lines aloud and have them checked against your script. This is an optional feature. How this is processed depends on your device's platform:

Android: Speech recognition is processed via Google's speech recognition service, which means audio is transmitted to Google's servers for the purpose of converting your speech to text. This is handled by Android's standard speech recognition API and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

iOS: Speech recognition is handled by Apple's Speech framework. Depending on your device and language, Apple may process the audio on-device or transmit it to Apple's servers to convert your speech to text. This is subject to Apple's own privacy policy.

On both platforms, OffBook does not receive, store, or retain any audio recordings or transcriptions beyond what is needed to check your spoken line in the moment.

4. AI-Powered Script Import & Scanning

OffBook offers optional AI features that turn a printed or digital script into rehearsable text more accurately than plain text extraction alone. These features are only used when you actively choose to import a file or scan pages, and they require an internet connection.

When you use these features, the script content you provide is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to be read and reformatted. Specifically:

  • Camera scanning — the page images you capture
  • Importing a PDF — the PDF file itself
  • Importing a Word (DOCX) or text file — the text extracted from that file

This data is transmitted to Google solely to convert it into OffBook's script format, and its use by Google is subject to Google's privacy policy. OffBook does not keep a copy of this content on any server — the formatted text is returned to your device and stored there.

If you are offline, or if the AI service cannot be reached, OffBook falls back to extracting the text entirely on your device (using on-device text recognition for camera scans, or built-in file parsing for documents), in which case nothing is sent to Google. You can also always type or paste a script in manually, with no network use at all.

5. Internet Access

OffBook requires internet access for the following limited purposes only:

  • The optional AI-powered script import and camera scanning features, which send your script content to Google's Gemini AI service (see Section 4)
  • Processing speech recognition via Google's speech recognition service when using the line rehearsal feature on Android devices (on iOS, this is handled by Apple's Speech framework — see Section 3)
  • Processing the optional one-off Pro unlock purchase via Google Play's billing system (Android) or the App Store's billing system (iOS)
  • Receiving app updates delivered through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store

Apart from the AI features you actively invoke and the purposes listed above, internet access is not used to transmit any personal data, script content, rehearsal data, or usage information. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are connected to this app.

6. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using your device's standard local storage. This includes:

  • Script and production data you enter or import
  • Rehearsal session history and confidence scores
  • App settings and preferences

This data is never transmitted off your device and is only accessible by the OffBook app.

7. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI. The optional script import and camera scanning features send your script content to Google's Gemini AI service for processing, as described in Section 4. This is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Billing. OffBook uses Google Play's billing system (Android) or the App Store's billing system (iOS) to process the optional one-off Pro unlock purchase. These transactions are handled entirely by Google or Apple respectively and are subject to their own privacy policies. OffBook does not receive or store any payment information.

Speech recognition. On Android, speech recognition is processed via Google's speech recognition service; on iOS it is handled by Apple's Speech framework, as described in Section 3.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

8. Children's Privacy

OffBook does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app does not collect personal data or build any profile of its users.
